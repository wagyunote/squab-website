Chapter I

珍貴的天然食材

Nature's Precious Ingredient

乳鴿肉厚而嫩，滋味鮮美，自古便是華人食療養生的珍品。民間素有「一鴿勝九雞」的美譽，可見其營養價值之高。

乳鴿含蛋白質最豐富，脂肪含量極低，消化吸收率高達 95% 以上，並含有 18 種氨基酸及多種微量元素。

隨著生活水平提升，消費者對安全優質的畜禽產品需求日益增加，乳鴿正成為現代人追求健康飲食的首選。

Chapter I

Squab meat is thick, tender, and delicious — treasured in Chinese dietary therapy for centuries. The saying "One pigeon beats nine chickens" speaks to its exceptional nutritional value.

Squab is richest in protein, extremely low in fat, with digestion absorption rate over 95%, containing 18 amino acids and various trace elements.

As living standards rise, more consumers seek premium, safe poultry products. Squab has become the preferred choice for modern wellness.
